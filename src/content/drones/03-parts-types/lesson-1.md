## Key Parts

- **Frame:** Structure of the drone
- **Motors:** Spins the propellers
- **Propellers:** Create lift
- **Battery:** Powers drone and attachments
- **Gimbal:** Stabilizes the sensor
- **Sensor/camera:** Collects data
- **Remote controller:** Pilot's control device

Each component plays a critical role in the drone's ability to fly, capture data, and complete agricultural missions successfully.

## Learning Activities

- Build a drone from kit
- Identify drone components and their functions
