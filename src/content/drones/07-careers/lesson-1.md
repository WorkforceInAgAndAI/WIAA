## The Global Challenge

- World population is increasing
- Available arable land is decreasing
- **Question:** How do we feed a growing population with less farmland?

## The Solution

Professionals in agriculture, AI, and technology

The intersection of these fields represents one of the most important career pathways for the 21st century. Companies across the agricultural technology sector are actively seeking skilled professionals who understand both farming and technology.

## Growing Industry

Major companies hiring in agricultural technology:

- John Deere
- Corteva Agriscience
- Bayer CropScience
- BASF
- Climate Corporation
- AgriTech startups nationwide

## Why This Matters

- Growing field = job security
- Competitive salaries
- Solve real-world problems
- Make a meaningful impact on global food security
